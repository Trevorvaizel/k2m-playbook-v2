## THE 4 HABITS: BRANDED FOR V2

### Habit 1: PAUSE BEFORE ASKING
**Tagline:** "Know what you want before you ask"
**Icon:** â¸ï¸ Pause button
**Zone Focus:** 0â†’1 (Wonder)
**Dual Pillar:** Democratized Intelligence (better thinking)

**What It Is:**
Before opening ChatGPT/AI, take 30 seconds to clarify: "What do I actually want from this interaction?"

**Why It Matters:**
- Transforms reactive impulse into intentional thinking
- Creates the "pause" that enables better prompting
- Serves the functional job: "Help me think better"

**Visible Evidence:**

- Self-assessment: "I can identify what I want before asking"
- Artifact shows clear question framing

## MEASURING HABIT ADOPTION (For Epic 6)

### Self-Assessment Questions (Weekly)

**Habit 1 (Pause):**
- "This week, did I pause before opening AI?"
- "Can I identify what I wanted before I asked?"

**Habit 2 (Context):**
- "Did I explain my situation before asking?"
- "Did AI respond better because I gave context?"

**Habit 3 (Iterate):**
- "Did I change one thing at a time when refining?"
- "What did I notice when I made each change?"

**Habit 4 (Thinking Partner):**
- "Did I use AI to think through a decision?"
- "How did AI help me clarify my thinking?"

### Proof-of-Work Verification (Decision 5)

After self-assessment, require:
> "Paste ONE sentence from your AI conversation that shows you used [Habit]."

**Example for Habit 2 (Context):**
> "Here's my sentence: 'I'm a sophomore struggling with chemistry, especially balancing equations. Can you help me understand the steps?' - This shows I explained my situation."
