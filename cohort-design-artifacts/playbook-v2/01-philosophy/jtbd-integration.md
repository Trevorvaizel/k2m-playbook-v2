## THE THREE JOBS WE'RE HIRED TO DO

From JTBD analysis (Decision 13), students hire K2M for three jobs:

### 1. Functional Job
> "Help me move from confusion to clarity in AI before university starts."

**Translation:** Give me mental models that work. Don't just teach tools—teach me how to think with AI.

### 2. Emotional Job
> "Help me stop feeling anxious and start feeling like I belong."

**Translation:** I'm surrounded by people who "get AI" and I feel left behind. Make me feel like this is for me, not just for "tech people."

### 3. Social Job
> "Give me proof I can show to myself, my parents, and my future."

**Translation:** I need evidence. I need to show my parents this was worth it. I need to believe I'm ready for university.

## ZONE-TO-JOB MAPPING: THE PHILOSOPHY INTEGRATION

### Zone 0 → "AI is not for me / it's sci-fi"

**Identity Shift:** From **outsider** to **observer**
**From:** "AI is something other people do"
**To:** "AI is something I witness happening"

**Jobs Served:**
- **Emotional:** "Maybe this isn't just for tech wizards" (relatability)
- **Social:** "People like me are using this" (belonging cues)
- **Functional:** "Oh, that's how it works" (clarity)

**4 Nodes (Wonder):**
1. "AI is not sci-fi—it's here" → **Witnessed experience** (Functional: clarity)
2. "People like me use this" → **Relatability mirror** (Emotional: belonging)
3. "I could try this" → **Permission + low-stakes entry** (Emotional: safety)
4. "It's actually fun" → **Emotional engagement** (Emotional: joy)

**Philosophy Statement:** "We start by making AI visible, relatable, and low-stakes. Wonder opens the door."

---

### Zone 1 → "AI is something I could use"

**Identity Shift:** From **observer** to **experimenter**
**From:** "I see AI happening"
**To:** "I can make AI do things"

**Jobs Served:**
- **Emotional:** "I'm not terrible at this!" (confidence)
- **Functional:** "It actually works for MY tasks" (utility)
- **Social:** "I can show people what I made" (proof)

**4 Nodes (Trust):**
1. "It actually works for MY tasks" → **Contrast experience** (Functional: utility)
2. "Low-stakes wins accumulate" → **Progressive confidence** (Emotional: pride)
3. "Errors are recoverable" → **Failure safety** (Emotional: psychological safety)
4. "I'm starting to rely on it" → **Habit formation** (Functional: integration)

**Philosophy Statement:** "We build trust through small wins, not impressive demos. Confidence comes from 'I did this,' not 'look what AI can do.'"

---

### Zone 2 → "AI does tasks for me"

**Identity Shift:** From **experimenter** to **collaborator**
**From:** "I make AI do things"
**To:** "AI and I work together"

**Jobs Served:**
- **Emotional:** "I have a thinking partner" (companionship)
- **Functional:** "Context changes everything" (understanding)
- **Social:** "I can explain my process" (articulation)

**4 Nodes (Converse):**
1. "Context changes everything" → **Vague vs. rich comparison** (Functional: insight)
2. "AI is a conversation partner" → **Mental model shift** (Functional: partnership)
3. "Explaining clarifies MY thinking" → **Meta-awareness** (Emotional: self-knowledge)
4. "We're getting closer together" → **Iterative refinement** (Functional: technique)

**Philosophy Statement:** "We shift from using AI to working WITH AI. Conversation replaces commands. Context becomes the superpower."

---

### Zone 3 → "AI understands my intent / I collaborate with it"

**Identity Shift:** From **collaborator** to **director**
**From:** "AI and I work together"
**To:** "I guide AI toward quality"

**Jobs Served:**
- **Emotional:** "I have opinions about quality" (ownership)
- **Functional:** "I can steer this toward what I want" (control)
- **Social:** "I made this" (authorship)

**4 Nodes (Direct):**
1. "First draft is raw material" → **Iteration mindset** (Functional: process)
2. "I have opinions about quality" → **Ownership emergence** (Emotional: standards)
3. "Direction techniques work" → **Skill acquisition** (Functional: capability)
4. "I made this" → **Identity shift to director** (Emotional: authorship, Social: proof)

**Philosophy Statement:** "We graduate from collaboration to direction. Students become authors, not just users. Quality becomes something they define, not just accept."

## PHILOSOPHY IMPLICATIONS: DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### Principle 1: Identity Before Competence
**What this means:** We prioritize identity shifts ("I'm someone who works with AI") over skill acquisition ("I know how to prompt").

**How it shows up:**
- Zone 0-1: Emphasize "people like me use this" (identity cue)
- Zone 2-3: Celebrate "I can explain my thinking" (identity evidence)
- Zone 4: Elevate "I made this" (identity claim)

**JTBD Connection:** Serves the **emotional job** of belonging and the **social job** of proof.

---

### Principle 2: Psychology Over Performance
**What this means:** We design for emotional safety and identity emergence, not outputs or speed.

**How it shows up:**
- Normalize confusion: "This is normal for your zone"
- Celebrate thinking: "Great context framing!" (not "Great answer!")
- Private process, public product (Discord hybrid model)

**JTBD Connection:** Serves the **emotional job** of anxiety reduction.

---

### Principle 3: Progress That Transfers
**What this means:** The transformation must extend beyond the cohort into university and life.

**How it shows up:**
- **4 Habits as graduation proof:** These work in ANY context
- **Zone 2-3 as valid outcome:** Journey A completion = success
- **Identity language:** "I'm someone who..." (not "I learned to...")

**JTBD Connection:** Serves the **functional job** of clarity before university + **social job** of future-readiness.

---

### Principle 4: Proof That Matters
**What this means:** The artifact proves identity transformation, not AI usage.

**How it shows up:**
- **Thinking Artifact structure:** Shows their thinking journey, not AI outputs
- **Proof-of-work requirement:** "Paste ONE sentence that shows AI understood YOU"
- **4 Habits graduation card:** Tangible evidence of identity shift

**JTBD Connection:** Serves the **social job** of proof for parents/future self.

## JTBD ALTITUDE SYSTEM (From Brand Framework v3)

**Integration with Brand Framework v3 (Decision 17):**

The philosophy we've documented must be expressed at different **abstraction altitudes** depending on the audience. This is not "dumbing down" - it's **respecting attention**.

**The 5-Level Altitude System:**

### Level 1 (Ground - Immediate Concrete)
- **Target:** Cold students (first touch)
- **Language examples:**
  - "You've done it: Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, submit"
  - "Sometimes AI works. Sometimes it doesn't."
  - "Stop feeling confused when you use AI"
- **Emotion:** Recognition ("I feel this")
- **Use for:** Zone 0-1 nodes, hooks, first-week content

### Level 2 (Pattern - Observable Truth)
- **Target:** Warm students, parents
- **Language examples:**
  - "Copy-pasters get stuck. Thinkers thrive."
  - "The habits you form now follow you forever"
  - "Learn WHEN to use AI and WHEN to think independently"
- **Emotion:** Pattern recognition
- **Use for:** Zone 1-2 nodes, student body copy, parent awareness

### Level 3 (Framework - System/Structure)
- **Target:** Parents, principals, engaged students
- **Language examples:**
  - "Mental models for WHEN/HOW/WHY to use AI"
  - "The cohort structure keeps them accountable"
  - "K2M teaches strategic AI integration"
- **Emotion:** Curiosity about the system
- **Use for:** Zone 2-3 nodes, method content, parent conversion

### Level 4 (Transformation - What Becomes Possible)
- **Target:** Principals, warm audiences
- **Language examples:**
  - "Students learn to think WITH AI, not just copy FROM it"
  - "Skills that compound across entire career"
  - "Build mental models that transfer to university"
- **Emotion:** Aspiration to transformation
- **Use for:** Zone 3-4 nodes, principal pitches, vision content

### Level 5 (Philosophy - Timeless Principle)
- **Target:** Believers, manifesto audience, internal alignment
- **Language examples:**
  - "AI is the democratization of expertise"
  - "Extension of consciousness"
  - "Revolutionary hope for amplified intelligence"
- **Emotion:** Wonder, mission-alignment
- **Use for:** Internal philosophy documents, brand manifesto, AFTER someone is already bought in

**The Golden Rule: START LOW, END HIGH**

For any content sequence:
- Touch 1 → Level 1-2 (Concrete/Pattern)
- Touch 2 → Level 2-3 (Pattern/Framework)
- Touch 3 → Level 3-4 (Framework/Transform)
- Touch 4 → Level 4-5 (Transform/Philosophy)
- **EARN the right to go higher**

**Key Principle:** Your philosophy can be Level 5. Your copy must match the JTBD altitude.
