## Executive Summary

### The Core Transformation

**FROM:** 8 human facilitators managing 200 students
**TO:** Agent-facilitated model with Trevor as 10% human layer + CIS thinking agents + node-based content

### The Product

A complete 8-week cohort program that guides students from "AI is for tech people" to "I am someone who thinks WITH AI" through:
- Zone-based identity transformation (Wonder ‚Üí Trust ‚Üí Converse ‚Üí Direct)
- 4 Thinking Habits (Pause ‚è∏Ô∏è, Context üéØ, Iterate üîÑ, Think First üß†)
- Thinking Artifact graduation deliverable
- CIS agents for structured thinking scaffolding

### Success Criteria

- 90%+ students reach Zone 2-3 (Trust ‚Üí Converse)
- 25-35% reach Zone 4 (Direct)
- 95%+ complete Thinking Artifact
- 95%+ earn 4 Habits graduation card
- Trevor time: ~2 hours/week (vs. 14+ hours/week in v1)
